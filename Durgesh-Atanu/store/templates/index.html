We are developer and Students of SPIT with our project AgroTech

By Durgesh Mandge with Atanu Debnath

This web based application is a complete package for farmers who are not much experienced in farming, have a small land and are living in the vicious cycle of middleman and vendors as well as retailers who wants to buy food essentials from anywhere to find good quality and price. This application:

  • It provides the recommendation of crop based on the soil testing and the weather conditions.
  • It provides live geolocation of farmer's land and based on that peasant can recieve weather forecasting warnings and messages as future scope of application.
  • It provides a platform that connects farmers directly to consumers or retailers thus, eliminates middlemen.
  • It can be used by both farmers as well as retailers/consumers accordingly for e-trading.
